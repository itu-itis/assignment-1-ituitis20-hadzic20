About Academic Side of School

Çapa Science Highschool is a state school that accepts 150 new students every year. It is always in top 10 best highschools list in Turkey. It accepts only the students that succeed in central highschool exams that are established by Turkish government. 44 succesful teachers are teaching the students of Çapa Science Highschool. Since it is a science highschool, its all students are getting education in MF(mathematics-natural sciences) field. That is why most of graduates are studying either medicine or engineering. There are also some graduates that choose studying social sciences after graduating from Çapa Science Highschool, which shows that its students are versatile. Also the school has great percents of university placements, in some years it gets the first place in this field between all Turkish highschools.

About Physical Side of School

Çapa Science Highschool has a campus founded on a big area. It is in center of city and has access to many transportation options. Some of features within campus area are:

  • School building
  • Dormitory

    • Female dormitory
  • Dining hall
  • Open sport areas

    • Basketball field
  • Benchs

School building which was build in 1901, but came into use as a school in 1951, has 4 floors. Some of its features are:

  • 20 classes
  • 3 laboratories
    • Physics
    • Chemistry
    • Biology
  • 1 library
  • 1 indoor gym
  • 1 conference hall
